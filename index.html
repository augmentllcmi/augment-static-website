Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services, approach, and engagement models.

How long does a typical cloud migration take?

Timeline varies based on complexity, but most migrations complete in 6-12 weeks. We start with a 2-week assessment phase to provide an accurate estimate. Our phased approach minimizes risk and ensures business continuity throughout the process.

Which cloud providers do you work with?

We specialize in AWS, Microsoft Azure, and Google Cloud Platform. Our team holds certifications across all three platforms and can help you choose the right provider based on your specific requirements, existing infrastructure, and business objectives.

What industries do you serve?

We work across multiple industries including healthcare, financial services, e-commerce, SaaS, and technology. Our experience spans HIPAA-compliant systems, PCI-DSS requirements, SOC 2 frameworks, and high-availability architectures.

How do you charge for your services?

We offer flexible engagement models: project-based fixed pricing for migrations and defined initiatives, monthly retainers for ongoing operations and optimization, and hourly consulting for assessments and advisory work. All engagements begin with a complimentary discovery call.

Can you guarantee specific cost savings?

While results vary by organization, our clients typically see 30-50% cost reduction in the first 90 days of optimization work. We conduct a thorough audit upfront and provide a detailed savings projection before engagement. Many clients achieve ROI within the first quarter.

Do you provide ongoing support after implementation?

Yes. We offer managed services, monthly retainers for continuous optimization, and on-demand support packages. Many clients start with implementation projects and transition to ongoing partnerships as their cloud operations mature.

What makes your AI enablement different from others?

We focus on practical, high-ROI use cases rather than experimental projects. Every AI implementation is tied to specific business metrics—reduced response times, increased throughput, lower operational costs. We prioritize quick wins that build momentum for larger initiatives.

How quickly can we get started?

Discovery calls are typically scheduled within 48 hours. After our initial consultation, we can begin assessment work within a week. For urgent situations (outages, security incidents, runaway costs), we can mobilize immediately.
